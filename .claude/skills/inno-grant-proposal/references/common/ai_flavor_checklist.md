# AI-Flavor Detection Checklist

Scan proposal drafts for these common AI-writing markers. Flag any found and provide specific location + concrete revision.

## English Markers (1-16)

1. Overuse of "delve," "underscores," "notably," "crucial," "comprehensive"
2. Excessive hedging ("may potentially," "could possibly")
3. Repetitive sentence openers ("This," "Furthermore," "Moreover")
4. Vague claims without specific evidence or numbers
5. Perfect parallel structure across all paragraphs (unnatural uniformity)
6. Absence of field-specific jargon (too generic)
7. Missing self-citations or PI-specific references
8. Formulaic transitions ("Building on this," "In light of")
9. Overly balanced pros/cons (real experts have opinions)
10. No acknowledgment of limitations or risks
11. Generic broader impacts (not specific to project/community)
12. Passive voice overuse in methodology
13. Missing numerical specifics (sample sizes, timelines, metrics)
14. Identical sentence length throughout (no rhythmic variation)
15. Over-promising ("transformative," "groundbreaking," "paradigm-shifting")
16. Lack of PI voice / personality (reads like anyone could have written it)

## Chinese (中文) Markers (17-24)

17. 过度使用"值得注意的是""需要指出的是""综上所述"
18. 反复出现"本研究旨在""本项目拟"等开头（正常出现1-2次，≥4次即为AI味）
19. 段落长度过度均匀（标准差过小，自然写作应有长短变化）
20. 缺乏领域隐性共识（domain-specific common sense只有圈内人才会写的内容）
21. 过度对称结构（如每个创新点都是"提出了…实现了…突破了…"的三段式）
22. 括号嵌套过多（如"（即…（也称…）…）"）
23. 缺少具体数据/基准引用（AI倾向于抽象描述而非引用具体数字/数据集）
24. 同源数据的自我验证循环（用生成方法A产出的数据去验证方法A）

## Scoring

- 0 items flagged: Clean — no action needed
- 1-3 items flagged: Minor — quick fixes during Phase 5
- 4-8 items flagged: Moderate — dedicated humanization pass needed
- 9+ items flagged: Severe — major rewrite recommended before submission
